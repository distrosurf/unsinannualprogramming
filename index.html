About UNSIN
Building community through the power of Latin music and culture.
UNSIN is a non-profit organization dedicated to elevating Latin music and its artists — creating year-round spaces for cultural expression, professional development, and community connection.
Community
Authentic spaces where Latin music and identity are centered, celebrated, and connected across generations and geographies.
Education
Equipping Latin music artists and professionals with the knowledge, tools, and networks to build sustainable, lasting creative careers.
Innovation
Research and support on the most pressing challenges with tech and AI in the creative economies — building pathways for Latin creators to lead, not just adapt.

Collaboration
Let’s build this together.
UNSIN welcomes organizations that share our commitment to Latin music, cultural equity, and community development. We design meaningful, values-aligned collaborations built around three core ideas.
Idea 01
Year-Round Programming
Present your organization across our full annual calendar — from UNSIN Festival in April through Talks and Labs. Consistent, meaningful visibility with Latin music audiences from February through December and beyond.
Idea 02
Community Building
Deepen your connection to Latin creative communities through authentic, co-created experiences at UNSIN Talks — from the New Year Series through Art Basel Edition. Four moments, one sustained relationship.
Idea 03
Education & Innovation
Align with UNSIN Labs at the forefront of AI and technology in the creative industries. Support the next generation of Latin creators navigating — and leading — the intersection of tech and culture.
What collaboration with UNSIN looks like
→ Access to Latin music audiences across multiple touchpoints throughout the year
→ Co-presented programming across Festival, Talks, and Labs
→ Visibility at in-person events reaching artists, fans, and industry professionals
→ Authentic connection with Latin artists, innovators, and community members
→ Custom collaboration models designed around your goals and our programs
→ Year-round presence across digital and in-person formats
→ Impact reporting and community outcomes documentation
